About Us

Preserving India's handloom heritage by connecting master artisans with conscious buyers worldwide.

Our Story

Handloom Marketplace was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sustainable digital platform that celebrates India's rich handloom heritage while ensuring fair compensation for the artisans who have preserved these traditions for generations.

What started as a small initiative in 2024 has grown into a thriving community of over 120 artisan clusters across 12 states, each bringing their unique craft stories, techniques, and cultural heritage to buyers who value authenticity and craftsmanship.

Our Mission

To empower handloom artisans by providing direct access to global markets while preserving traditional weaving techniques.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted platform for authentic handloom products, connecting conscious buyers with skilled artisans worldwide.

Our Values

Fair trade, transparency, sustainability, and respect for cultural heritage guide every decision we make.

Our Journey

Milestones that have shaped our growth and commitment to artisan communities.

2024
Platform Launch

Handloom Marketplace goes live with 25 artisan clusters from Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

2024
GI Tag Integration

Partnered with textile ministry to verify and display GI-tagged products on our platform.

2025
100+ Clusters

Reached the milestone of 100 artisan clusters across 10 states, expanding to include silk, cotton, and wool weaves.

2025
International Shipping

Launched worldwide shipping, connecting Indian artisans with buyers in 35+ countries.

2026
Sustainability Pledge

Committed to 100% carbon-neutral shipping and plastic-free packaging by 2027.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

Fair Trade

Ensuring artisans receive at least 70% of the product price, directly supporting their livelihoods.

Transparency

Every product includes detailed information about its origin, materials, and the artisans who made it.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly practices from natural dyes to zero-waste packaging initiatives.

Heritage

Preserving and celebrating India's diverse handloom traditions for future generations.
