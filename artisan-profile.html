About

Lakshmi Narayanan is a third-generation master weaver from Kanchipuram, specializing in authentic silk sarees. Her family has been practicing the art of handloom weaving for over 150 years, passed down through generations of skilled artisans.

She began learning the craft at age 10 under the guidance of her grandmother and has since mastered every aspect of traditional silk weaving - from thread preparation to the intricate zari work that makes Kanchipuram sarees world-renowned.

Lakshmi received the National Handloom Award in 2018 for her exceptional contribution to preserving and innovating traditional weaving techniques while adapting to modern design sensibilities.
